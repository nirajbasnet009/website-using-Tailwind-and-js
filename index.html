ABOUT US

Purwanchal Montessori & ECD Teachers Training Center is located in Birtamode, Jhapa, Nepal. It is a Montessori and Early Childhood Development (ECD) teachers' training center. It is a privately-owned center. The center provides Montessori and ECD teacher training courses. The courses are designed for those who want to pursue a career in teaching or who want to improve their teaching skills. The center offers certificate courses.

Purwanchal Montessori & ECD Teachers Training Center is dedicated to providing quality education to its students. The center has a team of qualified and experienced instructors who are committed to helping the students achieve their goals. The center also has well-equipped classrooms and a library. The center is committed to providing its students with the best possible education and training.
